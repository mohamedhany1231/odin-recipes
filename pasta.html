Lasagna

Ingredients

  • pasta
  • oil
  • tomato
  • garlic
  • salt

Steps

  1. Add garlic cloves, onion and bay leaf to it.
  2. Add water and then season with salt and sugar.
  3. Cover and let the tomatoes boil. Cool and then grind to a puree.
  4. Now, in a separate pan, heat oil and add chopped onions and chopped garlic.
  5. Add the tomato puree. Cook till its reduces to half. Add basil leaves and keep aside.
  6. Boil water with salt. Add the pasta to it and let it boil. Drain when done
  7. Transfer it to a serving dish and serve with the tomato sauce.
